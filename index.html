The red line at the center of the rink is used to determine icing violations. If a team shoots the puck from behind their own red line and it crosses the opponent's goal line without being touched, it results in an icing call.

What is a power play in hockey?

A power play occurs when one team has a numerical advantage due to an opponent serving a penalty. The team with the power play has more players on the ice.

In hockey, what is the penalty box?

The penalty box is an area where players serve time for infractions. When a player receives a penalty, they must leave the ice and sit in the penalty box for the specified duration.

What is a hat trick in hockey?

A hat trick is when a player scores three goals in a single game. It's a significant accomplishment and often celebrated by fans throwing hats onto the ice.
